Single Target Rotation


> > >

  • Keep Bloodthirst and Whirlwind on cooldown — never delay these abilities.
  • At excess rage, queue Heroic Strike to make your next main-hand auto attack a yellow attack.
    • Yellow attacks cannot produce Glancing Blows.
    • The game treats you as using only one weapon, removing the 19% dual-wield miss penalty from your off-hand swing.
    • The next main-hand swing will not generate any rage.
  • Use Hamstring as a filler when both Bloodthirst and Whirlwind are on cooldown.
    • Low damage yellow attack that cannot produce Glancing Blow.
    • It can critically hit and proc Flurry.
    • It can also proc Deep Wounds and Windfury.

Multiple Target Rotation


> > >

  • Keep Whirlwind and Bloodthirst on cooldown — never delay these abilities.
  • Whirlwind scales with your main-hand weapon and the number of enemies hit.
    • Whirlwind becomes more efficient than Bloodthirst in terms of damage-per-rage
      when hitting 2 or more targets (dual-wield or 2H), or 3+ targets when using daggers.
  • Always prioritize Bloodthirst on your main target.
  • At excess rage, queue Cleave to make your next main-hand auto attack a yellow attack.
    • Yellow attacks cannot produce Glancing Blows.
    • The game treats you as using only one weapon, removing the 19% dual-wield miss penalty from your off-hand swing.
    • The next main-hand swing will not generate any rage.
  • Use Hamstring as a filler when both Bloodthirst and Whirlwind are on cooldown.
    • Low damage yellow attack that cannot produce Glancing Blow.
    • It can critically hit and proc Flurry.
    • It can also proc Deep Wounds and Windfury.

At 20% HP or lower


>

  • The most efficient way to spend rage during the Execute phase is to use Execute immediately at the lowest possible rage cost.
  • With 2000+ Attack Power, Bloodthirst outperforms Execute due to better scaling.
    • On fights like Vaelastrasz the Corrupt, high-rage Executes still provide more value.
  • Switching to a fast weapon (1.5–1.8 atkspd) with similar DPS just before the Execute phase, known as Execute Switching,
    can improve damage-per-rage efficiency.
    • Less impactful in Classic Era than on private servers.
    • Still worth considering if the Execute phase lasts over 5 seconds. [ Source ]
  • Heroic Strike Cancelling is generally not considered a damage gain when using full consumables and world buffs.
    However, in theory, it should slightly improve off-hand damage and thus increase rage generation for stronger Executes.
    • Important: Mistiming this can delay an Execute, resulting in a significant damage loss.
